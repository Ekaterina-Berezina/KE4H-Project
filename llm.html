For Guercino‚Äôs Madonna col Bambino in gloria con san Pancrazio e una santa monaca, LLMs helped identify the painting‚Äôs correct location ‚Äî Chiesa di San Sebastiano in Renazzo ‚Äî which was previously missing from Wikidata.
